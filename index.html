About Pat's Salmon Cookies

We are dedicated to quality and sustainability. Our salmon cookies are made with wild-caught salmon and fresh local ingredients sourced from within Washington State. We use finely ground salmon along with whole wheat flour to create delicious goodies that you won't be able to get enough of! We also have gluten-free varieties. Our cookies are an essential source of omega-3s and protein; they are a balanced part of your daily diet, and they taste delicious!

Pat has been baking salmon cookies for over ten years. When she first started catering the cookies for weddings, they were a big hit and people could not believe that their main ingredient was salmon. Pat has decided to share her creations with the Greater Seattle Area, and she can't wait for you to try them! See below for some photos of what she offers at Pat's Salmon Cookies.
